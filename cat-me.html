Cat Me

A mobile app designed to help cat owners track health, behaviour, and care routines — with a focus on emotional connection and daily usability.

Role Lead Product Designer
Timeline —
Type Mobile App Design — iOS
Tools Figma, Adobe Photoshop

The challenge

The pet care app market is fragmented and generic. Cat owners struggle to find a tool built around the specificities of feline behaviour — daily rhythms, health tracking, and emotional signals.

  • No existing app combined health tracking with emotional connection
  • Interfaces were designed for dogs, with UX incompatible with feline behaviour patterns
  • Difficulty communicating health conditions to vets without structured data
  • No consistent design system across apps in the category

Solution

The goal was to design an app that centralised health, habits, and the cat's personality into a single interface — reducing daily friction and built to scale toward social and veterinary features.

  • Centralise health, habits, and personality in one interface
  • Warm but professional visual language — caring, not childish
  • Reduce friction for daily data entry
  • Expandable architecture toward social and veterinary features

UX Strategy

User Research

Main pain points: forgetting vet appointments, no clear behavioural history, difficulty assessing whether symptoms required a visit.

Information Architecture

4-tab structure: Today (daily diary), Health (vaccines, weight, medication), Profile (cat card), Explore (contextual tips). Every tab designed for one-handed use, maximum 3 taps for any action.

Interaction Design

Focus on micro-interactions to make the daily log fast and satisfying. Swipe gestures and quick-add patterns to reduce cognitive load.

Design System

Design system · palette, components, typography

Visual Language

Palette built on warm tones — terracotta orange, beige, warm white — communicating care and affection without feeling childish.

Core components: event card, health badge, vertical timeline, cat profile. Every component designed with hover, active, empty, and error states.

Key Screens

Today view · daily diary with quick-add
Health log · vaccine and weight history
Onboarding · cat profile setup
Profile · cat card with history

Impact & Outcomes

Results

User feedback: "finally an app that feels made for cats, not dogs."

Learnings

The main challenge was balancing functional depth with everyday simplicity. Version 1 had too many fields in the log — testing revealed users only engaged with 3 of the 7 available parameters.

The answer was an adaptive log system based on each owner's habits.
