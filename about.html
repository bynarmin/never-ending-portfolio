A little about me

Over the years I've worked across discovery, design, and delivery — translating complex problems into clear, buildable interfaces. I care deeply about the space between design intent and shipped product.

Recently I've been expanding my front-end skills to reduce the gap between design intent and shipped product, and to collaborate more deeply with engineering teams. I believe good design is invisible — it just works.

When I'm not in Figma, I'm probably reading about systems thinking, obsessing over typographic details, or watching the design community build tomorrow.
